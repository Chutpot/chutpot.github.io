## About
Guide Lumen, a mechanic with a gift for listening to old machines, as you uncover the stories hidden in the ruins. Solve spatial puzzles, tune resonant devices, and decide who to help as factions vie for control.

## Features
- Hand-crafted floating neighborhoods with vertical traversal
- Relationship-driven quests with branching outcomes
- Chill ambient soundtrack and minimalist UI
