## About
Every run, re-route vents and fuel lines to control how fire spreads. Snap decisions matter: heat unlocks powerful abilities but risks burning your exits.

## Features
- Momentum-first combat with directional dash attacks
- Procedural arenas with environmental hazards
- Meta-progression via forgeable ember cores
