## About

Embark on Cerebellum, a meticulously crafted 2D pixel art precision platformer set within the enigmatic confines of a vast spaceship. Navigate intricate levels designed to push your physical abilities to their limits. Each episode presents a unique set of challenges, demanding skillful jumps, dashes, and clever manipulation of the environment.

Unravel the mysteries of your surroundings as you progress through the ship. What is the purpose of these elaborate trials? What secrets lie hidden within the cold steel walls? Piece together fragments of information and discover the truth behind your journey through the stars.

Prepare for demanding gameplay that rewards patience and precision. Can you overcome every obstacle and reach the heart of Cerebellum?
